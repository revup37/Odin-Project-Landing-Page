Fun facts about Pikachu

  • Pikachu was based on a Japanese mochi snack called Daifuku
  • Pikachu loves ketchup!
  • Scientists discovered a new protein in 2008 and named it Pikachurin
  • "Pika" comes from the Japanese onomatopoeia "pika-pika" which means to sparkle, and "Chu" which is the sound a mouse makes in Japanese
